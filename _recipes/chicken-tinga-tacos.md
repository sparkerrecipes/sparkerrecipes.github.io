### Ingredients
* 1 1/2 pounds boneless chicken breasts or thighs
* 1 yellow onion, chopped
* 3/4 cup red enchilada sauce
* 2-3 chipotle peppers in adobo, finely chopped
* 1 teaspoon dried oregano
* 1 teaspoon ground cumin
* 1 teaspoon kosher salt
* 12-16 corn tortillas, warmed
* 1/2 cup shredded pepper jack cheese
* 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
* shredded lettuce and pickled onions, for serving

* 1/4-1/2 cup pickled jalape√±os

### Directions - Crock Pot
1. In the bowl of your crockpot, combine the chicken, onion, enchilada sauce, chipotle, oregano, cumin, and salt. Cover and cook on LOW for 4-5 hours or on HIGH for 2-3 hours.
2. When finished cooking, shred the meat and toss with the sauce. 

### Directions - Instant Pot
1. In the bowl of your instant pot, combine the chicken, onion, enchilada sauce, chipotle, oregano, cumin, and salt. Seal the lid and cook on high pressure for 8 minutes. Once done cooking, release the steam. 

### Directions - Crema
1. Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth and creamy. Season with salt.
2. Serve the tacos topped with avocado, lettuce, limes, and any other desired toppings.
