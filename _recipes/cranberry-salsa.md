### Source
[Pinch of Yum](https://pinchofyum.com/gingery-cranberry-salsa)

### Ingredients
* 1 12–ounce bag of fresh cranberries
* 1/3 cup sugar
* half of a jalapeño, ribs and seeds removed (see notes)
* one 1-inch knob of fresh ginger root, peeled
* 1 clove garlic
* 1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves
* 1/2 cup fresh parsley leaves
* juice and zest of 1-2 limes (to taste)
* 1/2 teaspoon salt (more to taste)

### Directions
0. Blitz cranberries in a food processor until broken down into a very finely chopped texture, being careful not to overly-chop or puree them. Transfer to a bowl and toss with sugar.
0. Add jalapeño, ginger, garlic, and cilantro to the food processor; pulse until finely chopped. Mix into the cranberry bowl; add lime juice, zest, and salt.
0. Refrigerate the salsa for 12-24 hours
