### Source
[Spend with Pennies](https://www.spendwithpennies.com/braised-chicken-thighs-with-mushrooms/)

### Ingredients
* 2 pounds bone-in skin-on chicken thighs
* ½ teaspoon seasoned salt or to taste
* black pepper to taste
* 1 tablespoon olive oil
* 1 tablespoon butter
* 1 pound mushrooms sliced ½" thick
* 2 teaspoons soy sauce
* 4 cloves garlic minced
* ½ teaspoon dried thyme leaves
* ½ teaspoon dried rosemary slightly crushed
* ⅔ cup chicken broth
* ½ cup white wine

### Directions
1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
1. Season chicken with salt & pepper. Set aside.
1. Heat butter and oil in a 10" skillet over high heat. Add chicken (skin side down) and brown without moving the chicken, about 4-5 minutes. Remove from pan and set aside.
1. Quickly toss the mushrooms with soy sauce. Cook mushrooms, garlic, thyme and rosemary until mushrooms are tender, about 5 minutes.
1. Add chicken broth and white wine. Simmer 2 minutes.
1. Place seasoned chicken, skin side up over the mushrooms.
1. Bake 35-40 minutes or until chicken reaches 165°F. Broil 1 minute if desired.
1. Spoon mushroom sauce over chicken. Garnish with parsley and serve.
