### Ingredients
* 1 c uncooked orzo pasta, wheat or gluten-free
* 1 cup chopped cherry tomatoes
* 1 clove minced garlic
* extra virgin olive oil spray
* 1 small red onion, quartered
* 1 red bell pepper, seeded and sliced into 4 pieces
* 1 yellow bell pepper, seeded and sliced into 4 pieces
* 1 large, 10 ounce zucchini, sliced into 1/4 inch thick rounds
* kosher salt
* black pepper, to taste
* 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
* 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
* 1/4 cup chopped basil
* feta (optional)

### Instructions
0. Chop the peppers, onion, and zucchini into small, but not diced, pieces. Arrange on a baking pan lined with foil. Drizzle with olive oil, salt, and pepper, then roast at 425 degrees for about 30 minutes, turning once.
0. Cook orzo in salted water according to package directions; drain and rinse under cold water, transfer to a large bowl and combine with tomatoes and 1 tablespoon of the oil and garlic.
0. Set aside on a cutting board to cool slightly, then dice into 1/2-inch pieces.
0. Toss with the remaining olive oil and vinegar and top with chopped basil. Taste and adjust vinegar as-needed.
0. Optionally, feta goes well in this.
