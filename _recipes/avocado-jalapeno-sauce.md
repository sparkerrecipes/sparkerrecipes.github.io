### Ingredients
* 1 avocado, peeled and seeded
* 1 jalapeño, stemmed
* ½ cup fresh cilantro leaves
* ½ cup water
* 2 cloves garlic
* 2 teaspoons white vinegar
* ½ teaspoon salt

### Directions
1. Combine all avocado-jalapeño sauce ingredients in the bowl of a food processor and process until smooth
